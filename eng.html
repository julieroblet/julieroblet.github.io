My method is based on onto-kinesiology

Once we have discussed the problem that drives you, I align myself with your Energy to enter into a more direct relationship with your body, its energy blocks and your 'memories'.

Through muscle testing and lists of information and other sources randomly chosen, we identify the sources of mental, emotional and physical blockages that prevent you from doing well, enjoying life and the others or that prevent you from achieving your goals.

Together we identify what has remained inscribed in your body, potential limiting beliefs,   forgotten injunctions and memories, being personal, familial or from a wider community.

By removing negative patterns that cause us to relapse into physical, psychic or emotional dysfunctions, we open the way to a lasting resolution through a better balance between the Conscious, the Unconscious and our Deep Nature.

Our Deep Nature aspires to Life and seeks to guide us in our journey. It cannot let us do something that does not go in the right direction, that is, in the direction of Life. To be heard, it can create an imbalance, to show that a transgression of a rule of life has occurred. This will encourage us to seek and understand, to find new solutions and to make new choices.

Bio

I was born in the 1970s, at the end of the '30 glorious years' and a stable and comfortable world: unemployment, divorces, the rise of populism, the technological revolution, Covid-19, global warming.

Little by little we have seen the concepts of progress and social justice crumble in favor of a  resigned and often soulless individualism that today permeates even the most intimate spheres of our lives and our psyche. It's not easy to grow and flourish in such an unstable world, especially when you are born hypersensitive as I am.

When I was young, I studied maths, physics, then economics and international politics, worked in the European institutions and then became involved in humanitarian causes and the defense of human rights in small and large organisations. I was a teacher and then trained in therapeutic counselling.

Curious about others and eager to discover the world, I traveled a lot and lived and worked abroad for a long time. I love difference, the human, cultural and spiritual encounter. My hypersensitivity and  different trying period of my life led me to psychotherapy. I got to know myself better to become more resilient. I learned to help others too.

I experienced onto-kinesiology during an episode of depression at the age of 40. In a few sessions, Nadia - who will become my trainer - helped me to get rid of a melancholy that had habited me from a very young age. By flushing out injunctions and thanks to the energetic rebalancing, I was able to unravel difficult situations and get closer to my Deep Nature.

Blown by its effectiveness, I then trained in the practice of releasing emotional and physical stress based on onto-kinesiology that Nadia has been teaching in Toulouse for 15 years.

This technique helps my interlocutors to go faster in the discovery of what makes them sick or what hinders them  in their search for feeling better, to open the field of possibilities, in harmony with their Deep Nature.
